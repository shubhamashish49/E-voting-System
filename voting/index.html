How the System Works

This system allows registered voters to cast their vote securely and privately, leveraging modern cryptographic methods like Shamir's Secret Sharing to ensure that votes are verifiable and tamper-proof.

After logging in with your EPIC number, you can cast your vote for your preferred candidate. Votes are securely stored and can be verified using a secret share process. Results are made available after the election ends, and only authorized personnel can view the results.
